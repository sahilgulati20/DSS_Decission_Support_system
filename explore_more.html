Our mission is to empower farmers with technology-driven solutions for sustainable farming.

What We Provide

Our platform offers farmers essential tools and information for enhancing productivity and sustainability.

Real-Time Weather Analysis

Our weather sensor provides real-time data to help farmers make informed decisions based on current conditions. Extreme weather warnings are sent via a buzzer sensor during events like heavy rain or storms.

Crop Price Tracking

Farmers can check average crop prices and also contribute by updating prices. This transparency keeps farmers informed about market trends.

Sustainable Farming Methods

To encourage eco-friendly practices, we provide resources and information on sustainable farming techniques.

Soil Nutrient Analysis

Our platform allows for both manual and sensor-based soil analysis, detecting key nutrients and providing suggestions on crop compatibility and soil improvement.

With our platform, farmers access practical, data-driven tools to boost productivity and adopt sustainable practices.
